The Amazon Purge Checker Reddit Bot, usually just called Purge Bot, is a script that scrapes data from Amazon's Top 10,000 Reviewers list and determines whose reviews were "purged" or reinstated on the current day and then posts that information to Reddit. Occasionally Amazon will remove all of a reviewer's reviews; this is referred to as "purging." It is usually done when a reviewer breaks Amazon's Terms of Service in some way.

This script was made in 2016 when Amazon was receiving backlash for its influx of incentivized reviews. Its goal was to see if Amazon was taking any kind of action against the issue.

Disclaimer: Purge Bot is not affiliated with Amazon in any way whatsoever.

Technologies used:

  • PHP
  • MySQL
