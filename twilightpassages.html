Twilight Passages

Twilight Passages is a small ghost hunting team based in New Jersey. I was tasked with both designing and developing a website for them. I tried to keep the site's design simplistic and dark without it feeling 'sinister' or 'gothic.' The design is also responsive and usable even on small smartphones. The backend uses Wordpress which was chosen so members of the Twilight Passages team could easily update the site's content themselves.

Technologies used:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • PHP
  • Wordpress
